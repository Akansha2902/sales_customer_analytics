# Sales & Customer Analytics Project
**Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing sales and customer data to uncover meaningful insights that can help improve business decisions. The analysis includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization to identify trends in customer behavior, product performance, and regional sales.

**Objectives**

Understand customer purchasing behavior
Identify top-selling products and categories
Analyze the impact of pricing and discounts
Explore city-wise sales distribution
Provide actionable business insights

**Tools & Technologies**

Python
Pandas
NumPy
Matplotlib
Seaborn
Jupyter Notebook

**Dataset**

The dataset contains:

Customer information
Product details
Sales transactions
Payment methods
City/location data

**Key Analysis Performed**

Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
Handling missing values
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Sales trend analysis
Customer segmentation basics
Visualization of patterns and insights

**Key Insights**

Most customers purchase 1–2 items per transaction
Mid-to-high priced products are preferred
UPI is the most commonly used payment method
Electronics is the top-performing category
Chairs and tablets are among the best-selling products
Cities like Pune, Chennai, and Bangalore show higher sales activity

**Business Recommendations**

Focus marketing on high-demand product categories
Optimize inventory for top-selling products
Target high-performing cities for expansion
Offer strategic discounts to increase sales volume
Enhance digital payment experiences

**Learning Outcomes**

Improved data cleaning and preprocessing skills
Gained experience in exploratory data analysis
Learned to extract actionable insights from data
Enhanced data visualization techniques
